The Garden Planner is here to assist you in the development of your very own personal garden. With this site you will be able to enter in your zip code that will list the various plants that is plantable within your zone and will be added to a plant report based off what you choose to add. The Garden Planner understands that having a garden can be difficult to plan. Especially, if you do not have the time for research and planning. No worries, with my help you will be able to easily pick what you would like to plant and be able to craft a schedule. Easy Peasy.
